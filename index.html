AI Leap user journey

Bring an AI idea to life

Teams start with a real problem, shape it into a credible AI use case, then use AI Leap to assess readiness and route the idea to the right support path.

Journey logic

Do not choose the route upfront

The support path depends on readiness, urgency, support need, theme fit, team capacity, data route and ownership.

Leap StudioDefault build route for ready use cases that need focused support now
Leap HackathonOccasional cohort route when the idea fits a theme, timing and visibility need
SME or self-serveShaping route when the idea has potential but is not yet sprint-ready

Step 1

Spot opportunity

Identify a real problem before thinking about the route.

User question

Where could AI help us solve a real client, delivery or internal problem?

Team action

Name the pain point, who it affects, why it matters and what would improve if solved.

AI Leap role

Not involved yet. The team is still identifying whether there is a meaningful opportunity.

Output

A short opportunity statement that can be shaped into a use case.

3a

AI Leap Hub

The team actively looks for where to take its idea and lands on the Hub.

3b

Hackathon announcement

The team sees an email, Teams post or leadership message about an upcoming event.

3c

Referral

An SME, partner, project lead or colleague points the team toward AI Leap.

Core message

AI Leap is not asking teams to pick a process. It helps teams test readiness and route the idea to the right level of support.
